Profile & Prowess

Jacob Njoku is a Nigerian professional forward known for explosive movement, clinical finishing, and strong hold-up play. He has featured in Malaysian football with Sabah FC and Kelantan United, and currently plays for Kasuka FC (Brunei, joined Aug 2025). His instincts in the box and work-rate make him a consistent goal threat.
